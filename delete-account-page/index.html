Account and Data Deletion

If you want to delete your account or request the removal of your personal data associated with this app, please follow the steps below:

  • Send an email to: support@yourdomain.com
  • Use the subject line: "Account Deletion Request"
  • Include in your email:
    • The Google email address you used to sign in
    • The name of the app

Once we receive your request, we will delete your account and associated data within 7 business days.

If you have any questions, feel free to contact us at the same email address.
